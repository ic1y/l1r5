Some Notes

  • Your L1R5 must not exceed 20, and meet grade requirements for specific subjects to be eligible for admission to a junior college. Grade requirements being:
    • A1 to C6 for English
    • 1-7 for MTL or 1-8 for Higher MTL or Merit or Pass for 'B' Syllabus
  • The R4 and R5 subject can be any GCE O-level subject, except for Religious Knowledge.
  • There should be no repetition of subjects (no shit).
  • Input your numerical grade, meaning if you scored an A2, you just input "2".
